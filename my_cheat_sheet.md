### Nice to have commands
* date
  * print out the time of the system
* ncal 
  * print out a vertical calander of current month
  * print out the calendar of the entire year: 
    ```s
    ncal 2025
    ```
  * print out the calendar of a given month in a given year
    ```s
    ncal jan 2022
    ```
